Camp Games

Designing games for 100+ University students.

Honestly, this was an opportunity to put my diabolical mind to work while also designing stuff.

2018: "The Suitcase Game"

The game was simple: Solve the puzzles, unlock the chests (suitcases) and complete "the task". With that, they launched into 2 hours of what was later dubbed the "Suitcase Game".

It had everything. A Middle Earth style map, ridiculous challenges, secret clues and a fearsome man chasing you as you tried to steal his toilet paper.

2019: "SAWnopoly"

Combine Monopoly and the SAW franchise and you get this torturous game. University campuses compete against each other and their leaders have to perform punishment challenges if they lose. Even Jigsaw dropped by to help us MC.

2019: "The Greatest SOWman"

We hosted a skit competition which combined a popular movie and a genre. For example: Cinderella and Horror. Results: Amazing.
